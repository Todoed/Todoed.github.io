Todoed Alpha

Available in theChrome Web Store

Turn any selected text into a delegated todo, with just one right click.

Todoed is a new kind of a seamless todo listing tool, assisting the user on any text spotted anywhere on the web browser and (soon), any device, as if that text is a delegate-able todo item first.

Seamless? Yes! You don't have to switch screens or apps, meaning, you don't even have to open Todoed to use it to delegate to yourself or your coworkers. Delegate from wherever you are on your browser or (soon) anywhere on any device. When you have a thought or a task, you should be able to instantly not only share it as an instant message or an email or whatever has been your previous method of sharing it, you should also be able to delegate it at the same instance of sharing it. Seamlessly.

Todoed makes a perfect productivity companion for

Mark any text as a to-do

Clip text from anywhere on the web & (soon) mobile too to mark anything as a todo.Works perfectly with emails, facebook groups, yammer; absolutely any where on the browser.

Assign tasks and collaborate

Todoed let's you mark any text, any where, any time and delegate it instantly to your coworker's.

Make chats more productive and accountable

Being able to use it with facebook messenger has been our own favorite use case of Todoed. It made fb messenger more productive and accountable for us. Todoed will do the same for you too.

Record your own thoughts

Open the browser extension , type and (soon) voice record your thoughts instantly. Todoed to yourself or delegate it in a jiffy.

Have you ever felt an urge that you should create a to do list for yourself but haven't yet started? Download Todoed and you will find yourself started already.
