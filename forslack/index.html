Todoed Alpha

Available in theChrome Web Store

Turn any selected text into a delegated todo, with just one right click.

Todoed is a new kind of a ​seamless todo listing tool, that clips any selected text anywhere on the web browser and (soon), any device, as if that text is an actionable, delegate-able and/or bookmark-able todo item first. Thus, increasing the productivity and accountability of that communicated text.

When you have a thought or a task or a web discovery, you should be able to instantly not only share it as an instant message or an email or whatever has been your previous method of sharing it, you should also be able to delegate it at the same instance of sharing it, from where ever you are on your device. Seamlessly. Invisibly. Simply.

Seamlessly? Yes! You don't have to switch screens or apps, meaning, you don't even have to open Todoed to use it to delegate to yourself or your coworkers. Delegate from wherever you are on your browser or (soon) anywhere on any device.

Invisibly? Yes! Todoed is the only tool with design and purpose of being an invisible underlying layer in your daily productivity. Use it to know how it really doesn't come in the way of your work flow, whatever it was or will be, before or after Todoed. It is not trying to compete with other productivity tools out there. It is simply becoming an invisible layer before them all. Other productivity tools want you to open their apps to help you be productive. Todoed doesn't. Todoed wants to help you without inducing you to open the main Todoed app. However, if you open Todoed, it will help you even more.

Simply? Doesn't it seem like the simplest productivity solution in the world? ​It is. ​
